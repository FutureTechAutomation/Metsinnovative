What We Use
Materials Available

We stock premium filaments to match every application — from flexible gaskets to high-temp industrial parts.

🟢
PLA
Best for decorative, prototyping, and general use. Biodegradable, easy to print, vibrant colors.
🔵
PETG
Strong, slightly flexible, chemical resistant. Ideal for functional parts, enclosures, food-safe prints.
⚫
ABS
High temp resistance, tough, machinable. Used for automotive, electronics housings, and industrial parts.
🟡
TPU
Flexible rubber-like filament. Great for gaskets, phone cases, shock mounts, drone bumpers.
⬜
ASA
UV and weather resistant. Perfect for outdoor applications, signage, and automotive parts.
🔴
PA / Nylon
High strength, wear resistant, low friction. Used for gears, bearings, structural mechanical parts.
